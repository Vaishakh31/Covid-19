HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

Made with love by By ProGrad Jr ❤️ Vaishakh AR

Wash your hands regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanditizer for 30
seconds will help you and will keep you away from germs

Cover Your Mouth & Nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or
coughing in public. Even better use a mask

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded places. Prefer
staying indoors

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming Junk food. Eat as healthy as possible.
To boost your immunity

Stay Calm

Regular exercise or meditation is a great way to keep
yourself calm and composed

Be Aware.Be Safe
